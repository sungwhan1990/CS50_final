How hot is it outside?

(Image: https://www.ibtimes.com/us-heat-wave-map-2019-list-states-affected-excessive-hot-weather-2807286)

According to the CDC, since 1999, 630 people have died annually because of heat-related symptoms in the US.

The number of death almost doubled over the last decade, and more people are getting affected by the heat waves.

This project aims to inform when it is a dangerous time to stay outside and how our future will be using the metric heat index.

The increasing risk in our future is closely related to climate change can carbon emission.

How can we stay safe and reduce the hazard from the heat?
